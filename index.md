## Common Questions

**Does my streak sync across devices?**
No — streaks are stored locally on your device.

**How do I restore my premium purchase after reinstalling?**
Open Settings and tap "Restore Purchases."

**Can I get a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
